Part 7: Recommendations

7.1 Before You Launch — Do This First (4-8 weeks)

1. Test with Pop-Up (₹2-3L investment)

  • Rent weekend space at a premium location
  • Run for 4 weekends, track # customers, average bill, repeat rate
  • KEY METRIC: If you can't get 30-40 customers on Saturday, the permanent store will struggle

2. Pre-Launch Membership Sales (₹0 investment)

  • Sell 100 founding memberships at ₹15,000/year
  • Generates ₹15L upfront capital + validates demand
  • KEY METRIC: If you can't sell 100 memberships, market isn't ready

3. Reduce Initial Capital (if budget is limited)

  • Start with 800-1,000 sq ft instead of 1,500
  • Simpler interior (₹12-15L vs ₹25L)
  • Smaller inventory (₹3-4L) — Saves ₹15-20L
